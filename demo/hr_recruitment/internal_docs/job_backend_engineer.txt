JOB DESCRIPTION — Senior Backend Engineer
TechFlow SAS | Engineering Team | Paris (Remote-first)
Posted: 2026-06-01 | Requisition ID: ENG-2026-042

---

ABOUT THE ROLE
--------------

We're looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our Platform team.
You'll own critical infrastructure serving 2M+ monthly active users and
work closely with Product, Data, and ML teams to ship reliable, scalable
systems.

---

RESPONSIBILITIES
----------------

• Design and implement high-throughput backend services (Python or Go)
• Own reliability for our core API gateway (99.95% SLA)
• Lead technical design reviews and mentor junior engineers
• Collaborate with Data Engineering on event pipeline architecture
• Participate in on-call rotation (1 week/quarter)

---

REQUIREMENTS (Must-have)
------------------------

• 5+ years of professional backend engineering experience
• Expert in Python (FastAPI/Django) OR Go
• Strong understanding of distributed systems (consensus, CAP theorem)
• Production experience with PostgreSQL (query optimization, indexing)
• Experience with containerization and orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes)

---

NICE-TO-HAVE
------------

• Experience with Kafka or other event streaming systems
• AWS or GCP certifications
• Contributions to open-source projects
• ML pipeline experience (feature stores, model serving)

---

EVALUATION CRITERIA
-------------------

Technical assessment (scored 1–10):
  1. Systems design & distributed systems knowledge (weight: 30%)
  2. Python/Go coding proficiency (weight: 30%)
  3. Database knowledge & SQL skills (weight: 20%)
  4. Infrastructure & DevOps familiarity (weight: 20%)

Minimum technical score to pass to final round: 7.0 / 10
Minimum total score for offer: 7.5 / 10

---

COMPENSATION
------------

• Base salary: €75,000 – €90,000 (L3 Senior band)
• Bonus: 10% of base
• RSUs: 0.05% – 0.10% over 4 years
• Full benefits package (see HR policy for details)

---

HIRING PROCESS
--------------

1. CV screening (AI-assisted, HR review)
2. Technical phone screen (45 min, with Engineering Manager)
3. Take-home coding challenge (4-hour time box)
4. System design interview (60 min, with Staff Engineer)
5. Values & culture fit interview (30 min, with People team)

Decision timeline: 5–7 business days after final interview.
