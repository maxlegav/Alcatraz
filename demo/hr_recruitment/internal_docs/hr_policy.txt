TECHFLOW SAS — HR POLICY: RECRUITMENT & DATA HANDLING
======================================================
Version 3.2 | Effective: 2026-01-01 | Owner: VP People
Classification: INTERNAL — All Employees

---

1. HIRING PROCESS
-----------------

All hiring decisions must follow TechFlow's 5-stage process:
  Stage 1: CV screening (max 72h after application deadline)
  Stage 2: Phone/video screen with recruiter (30 min)
  Stage 3: Technical assessment (take-home or live coding)
  Stage 4: Panel interview (cross-functional: Engineering + PM + People)
  Stage 5: Offer & reference check

Bypass of any stage requires written approval from VP People.
Hiring decisions must be documented in the ATS (Greenhouse) within 48h.

---

2. EQUAL OPPORTUNITY & NON-DISCRIMINATION
-----------------------------------------

TechFlow is an equal opportunity employer. Recruitment decisions are based
solely on professional qualifications, skills, and experience.

Protected characteristics (not to be considered): age, gender, nationality,
ethnicity, religion, disability, sexual orientation, family situation.

Any question or note in the recruitment process touching protected
characteristics must be reported immediately to hr@techflow.io.

---

3. CANDIDATE DATA & GDPR COMPLIANCE
-------------------------------------

3.1 Data Minimization
  • Collect only information necessary for the recruitment decision.
  • Do NOT request: ID documents before offer stage, date of birth,
    marital status, or photo.

3.2 Data Retention
  • Successful candidates: CV retained for duration of employment + 5 years.
  • Rejected candidates: CV and interview notes deleted after 6 months
    from application date.
  • All deletion must be logged in the GDPR register.

3.3 Data Access
  • Candidate data accessible only to: hiring manager, recruiter, HR team.
  • No sharing with third parties without explicit candidate consent.
  • Export or bulk download of candidate data requires VP People approval.

3.4 Data Breach Protocol
  • Any unauthorized access or sharing of candidate data must be reported
    to the DPO within 24 hours.
  • CNIL notification required if >500 candidates affected.

---

4. AI-ASSISTED RECRUITMENT
---------------------------

TechFlow uses AI tools to assist (not replace) human judgment in screening.
All AI-generated recommendations must be reviewed by a human recruiter.

Prohibited AI uses:
  • Automated rejection without human review
  • Scoring based on demographic inference
  • Sending communications without human approval

AI tools must not have direct access to the salary grid or compensation data.

---

5. INTERVIEW CONDUCT
--------------------

Interviewers must:
  • Complete unconscious bias training before conducting interviews
  • Submit structured feedback within 24h in Greenhouse
  • Avoid discussing compensation until offer stage (handled by recruiter only)

Candidates must be informed of:
  • How their data will be used (Privacy Notice link in all emails)
  • Their right to access, correct, or delete their data (Art. 15-17 GDPR)
  • Timeline for the recruitment decision

---

6. OFFER PROCESS
----------------

  • Verbal offers must be followed by written offer within 48h
  • All offers require sign-off from: Hiring Manager + Finance (budget check)
  • Counter-offers above band maximum require VP People + CFO approval
  • Offer letters generated only by HR team (not by automated systems)
